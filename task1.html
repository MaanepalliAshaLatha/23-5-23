PERSONAL INFO:

Arijit Singh (born 25 April 1987) is an Indian singer and music composer. The recipient of several accolades including a National Film Award and seven Filmfare Awards, he has recorded songs in several Indian languages. He is also known for his humble personality and simple lifestyle. Singh began his career when he participated in the contemporary reality show, Fame Gurukul in 2005, but did not receive widespread recognition until the release of "Tum Hi Ho" and "Chahun Main Ya Naa" in 2013.
He was declared the most-streamed Indian artist of 2020, 2021 and 2022 by Spotify. He is the most followed Asian soloist on Spotify.
When he was 10 years old, he got a scholarship from the state government of West Bengal for his training in Indian Classical Music. Growing up, he listened to Mozart, Beethoven, and Bengali Classical Music.Arijit was born in a Punjabi Family. His father was a LIC agent, and his mother, Aditi Singh, was a homemaker who succumbed to a cerebral stroke on 19 May 2021 at AMRI Hospital in Dhakuria, Kolkata. She was 52 years old when she breathed her last. She was admitted to the hospital with the COVID-19 symptoms; however, on 17 May 2021, she tested negative for the disease.He has a sister named Amrita Singh.He married twice.
His first wife, Ruprekha Banerjee, was one of the finalists of the reality show Fame Gurukul. They married in 2013.However, the marriage lasted only for a short time. He married Koyel Roy Singh in 2014, who is the childhood sweetheart of Arijit. Arijit Singh is the second husband of Koyel Roy Singh.The couple has three children, two sons, one of his son’s name is Jul and a step-daughter from Koyels first marriage.Arijit Singh has the net worth of about $7 million.
